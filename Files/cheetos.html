Cheetos Recipe


  • First, Boil the potatoes.
  • Then make cheetos batter with the help of grinder
  • Add Corn starch ( 6 to 7 table spoon ) into the cheetos batter
  • Add salt as per taste
  • Add Red Chilli Powder 1/4 Tea Spoon
  • Add Black Pepper Powder 1/4 Tea Spoon
  • Mix it well and add some oil in it and again mix it
  • Use Zip Loc or Piping Bag to fill the potato Batter
  • Make a small hole at the bottom of the Zip loc or piping bag
  • Deep fry for 3 to 4 minutes on medium heat
  • Fry until it turns into crispy golden brown
  • Add a pinch of Black pepper powder and cheetos are ready to eat
